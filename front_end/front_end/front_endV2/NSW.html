Sydney

In NSW, winter crops commonly include cereals such as wheat, barley, oats and triticale; oilseeds such as canola, mustard and safflower and

pulses such as lupins, chickpeas, fababeans and fieldpeas.

The Winter crop variety sowing guide helps to select the most suitable variety and contains updated technical information from the latest

research, extension and industry programs.
